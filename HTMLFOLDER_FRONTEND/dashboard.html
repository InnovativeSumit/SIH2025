Our Story

Founded in 2025, CROPNEXUS began as a university project aimed at helping local farmers improve their crop yields. Today, we've grown into a comprehensive platform serving farmers worldwide.

Our Technology

CROPNEXUS uses advanced machine learning algorithms combined with satellite imagery and IoT sensor data to provide accurate predictions and recommendations tailored to your specific farming conditions.

Frequently Asked Questions

How accurate are the crop predictions?

Our crop prediction algorithm has an accuracy rate of approximately 85-90% based on historical data and field testing. Accuracy may vary based on the quality of input data and local conditions.

Is my data secure with ALCROP?

Yes, we take data security seriously. All your farm data is encrypted and stored securely. We never share your personal information or farm data with third parties without your permission.

What equipment do I need to use ALCROP?

ALCROP works with any internet-connected device. For optimal results, we recommend using our soil sensors which provide real-time data to the platform. Basic functionality is available with just manual input of your farm conditions.

How often is weather data updated?

Weather data is updated every 3 hours from reliable meteorological sources. Severe weather alerts are pushed to users in real-time.

Can I use ALCROP on my mobile device?

Yes, ALCROP is fully responsive and works on smartphones and tablets. We also have mobile apps available for iOS and Android for enhanced functionality.
